Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I register for an event?

A: You can register by filling out the form on the Registration page. Please make sure to provide all the required details.

Q: Is there a fee to participate?

A: No, participation is free for all registered students. Some workshops may have a nominal fee for materials.

Q: Can I participate in multiple events?

A: Yes, you can. However, be aware of the event schedules to avoid any conflicts with timing.

Q: What should I bring to the event?

A: We recommend bringing your college ID, a laptop (for coding events), and a notebook. Specific requirements for each event will be communicated to registered participants.
